About Us

Welcome to Laddu Fish Market

Fresh fish refers to seafood that has not been frozen or preserved in any way before reaching the consumer. It is typically caught and sold within a short period, ensuring maximum flavor, texture, and nutritional value. Fresh fish is often preferred for its superior taste and quality compared to frozen or processed alternatives. When selecting fresh fish, it's essential to consider factors like the fish's appearance (bright, clear eyes; firm flesh; and a clean, briny smell), as well as where and how it was caught to ensure sustainability and minimize environmental impact. Proper storage and handling are also crucial to maintain freshness and prevent spoilage.

Whether you're a seafood enthusiast or a culinary connoisseur, we offer a wide selection to cater to every taste and preference. From succulent salmon to mouthwatering mackerel, our diverse range includes all your favorite varieties and more. Explore our vibrant marketplace and discover the finest seafood experience at Laddu Fish Market today!
